About

Motivation:

Personally, I like traveling around, and when I visit anywhere, I like going to the local art museums and displays. For me, that would be one of the best ways to know about the culture and art atmosphere of that city. Also, even when staying in San Francisco, there are various displays at different times. Therefore, I think visualization of arts on displays on a map would really help people who wants to know what and where they could go for art displays that they might be interested in.
