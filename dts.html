Dataset: Civic Art Collection by Arts Commission

Link to the dataset: Civic Art Collection

This data set includes all of the publicly-sited works in the Civic Art Collection, which includes historic monuments, murals, and artworks commissioned through the City's Public Art Program. The data set includes the following categories: artist name, title of work, medium, dimensions and location.

Size: 942 Rows; 20 Columns

License: Open Data Commons Public Domain Dedication and License

Source Link: http://www.sfartscommission.org

Retrieve Date: April 11, 2020

Relevant Columns:

  1. Creation Date: Date of creation of this item.
  2. Artist: Artist who created this art item. Categorical variable.
  3. Display Title: Title of display of this art item. Categorical variable.
  4. Medium: Material or texture of this art item. Categorical variable.
  5. Facility: Facility that in charge of this art item. Categorical variable.
  6. Current Location: Display department of where the art item. Categorical variable.
